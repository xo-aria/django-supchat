## ❌ What You CANNOT Do

### 1. Rebrand Without Attribution
You **cannot**:
- ❌ Rename SupChat to something else without mentioning "SupChat"
- ❌ Remove all references to the original project
- ❌ Claim you created SupChat

**Examples of violations:**
- ❌ Renaming to "MyChat" with no mention of SupChat
- ❌ "Built from scratch by [Your Name]" (when it's based on SupChat)
- ❌ Removing copyright notices

### 2. Imply Endorsement
You **cannot**:
- ❌ Suggest xo-aria endorses your fork/extension
- ❌ Use "Official SupChat" unless authorized
- ❌ Use SupChat logos without permission

### 3. Use in Confusing Ways
You **cannot**:
- ❌ Create a competing product called "SupChat Pro" without clear distinction
- ❌ Use SupChat branding in a way that confuses users about the source
